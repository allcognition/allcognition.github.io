Technology for Bettering Your Brain

Transcaranial direct current stimulation, also know as tDCS, is a non-invasive therapy that involves the delivery of constant and low current to the brain. Over the last decade, there have been several studies that suggest that tDCS can be used to enhance the cognitive function of the brain. One of the studies conducted by Defence Advanvced Research Project Agency (DARPA), shows that tDCS was able to cut the learning time of subjects in half, doubling their learning speed.
